Music X is a sleek, immersive music player with Material 3 UI, custom themes, and powerful audio features. It is an open-source application that does not require registration, and all content is free for users.

Lyrics Support: View synchronized song lyrics while listening to your music for an enhanced experience.

Equalizer: Customize the sound with a powerful built-in equalizer and presets to match your preferences.

Swipe Gesture Control: Control music playback effortlessly using intuitive swipe gestures.

Material You & Dynamic Colors: Experience a smooth, modern UI with dynamic color support for a personalized look.

Fade-in/Fade-out Transitions: Enjoy seamless track changes with smooth audio transitions.

Floating Mini Player: Multitask with a floating music widget that keeps your playback accessible at all times.

Sleep Timer: Set a timer to automatically stop playback when you sleep.

Local Music Management: Organize your songs by album, artist, genre, and folder for easy access.

Audio Boost & 3D Sound: Enhance your listening experience with advanced audio effects.

Lockscreen & Home Widget: Control music directly from your lock screen or home screen widget.

Music X is designed to give you the best listening experience with powerful features and a stunning UI.
