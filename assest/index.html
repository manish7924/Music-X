Music X

A simple offline music player that lets you play your device’s stored songs effortlessly.

Screenshots

Description

Open-Source application that does not require registration and all content is free for users.

Here are some features of this application

Offline Playback: Enjoy your favorite songs stored on your device without needing an internet connection.

Lyrics Support: View song lyrics while listening to your music for an enhanced experience.

Equalizer: Customize the sound with a built-in equalizer to suit your preferences.

Swipe Gesture Control: Control music playback effortlessly using intuitive swipe gestures.

Material you & Dynamic Colors: Experience a smooth, modern UI with dynamic color support for a personalized look.
